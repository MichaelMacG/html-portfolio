Movies That I Love

No particular order, they're all Brilliant!

One boy and his dog

Post-apocalyptic and one of my favourite movies growing up. Definitely weird in places and is listed as a cult classic. Without spoiling the movie it's essentially based on the world being nuked, with a few human survivors and loads of mutants/ cannibals running around trying to eat the main hero and his telepathic dog.

In my opinion the dog is the main hero, due to it's heightened senses it can smell these cannibals from miles away and is constantly saving the human boy from certain death.

The Last Samurai

I've always been a fan of movies involving the far east, whether it be Chinese subbed martial art movies or amazing Anime's. This captured a period of history I was fascinated with, just after the American Civil war and captured brilliantly the dying age of the Samurai. Controversial choice I know, as a lot of people i've mentioned this movie to have let me know they thought it was absolute rubbish, but I love it, so their opinion is void.

Spirited Away

One of the first Anime movie's I watched as a kid, and I must have seen it well over 50 times, probably more. This sparked my love of Anime movies and to this day I still think it's a masterpiece.

Red Cliff

Based on the battle of Red Cliffs, this is an amazing movie. Honestly one my favourite martial arts/war movies of all time. The story is beautifully done and the scenes and the whole movie is nothing short of epic. Only ever watched the subbed version as I refuse to watch dubbed versions because I find the dubbed voices never really hold a candle to the originals.
